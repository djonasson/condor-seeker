# Possible Features

## Core Rounds and Scoring

- Digital scorecard for stroke play and Stableford, sometimes match play and other formats.
- Configurable scoring modes - Beyond stroke/Stableford/match, support modified Stableford, best-ball, scramble, alternate shot, and Chapman/Pinehurst formats.
- Scoring for full group (you and your foursome) in one app.
- Gross and net scoring with automatic net calculation per hole and per round.
- Live scoring during the round, with easy "add score" flows from the GPS screen.
- Voice scoring - Hands-free input while walking (e.g., "Hey Condor, birdie on 7"). Avoids pulling out your phone mid-round.
- Penalty/provisional tracking - Tap where the ball went OB or into water; the app auto-applies stroke-and-distance or lateral relief rules and adjusts the score.

## GPS and On-Course Tools

- GPS rangefinder with distances to front/center/back of green, hazards, and layup targets for 40k-45k+ courses worldwide.
- Hole maps with 2D/3D flyovers or previews to visualize each hole.
- Watch apps (Apple Watch, Wear OS) showing distances and enabling on-wrist scoring and shot tracking.
- Optional green maps for slopes/breaks on many courses (often a paid/pro feature).
- Extra GPS widgets/tools such as distance arcs, compass, pace-of-play timer, notes, camera, and in-round games/leaderboard widgets.
- Wind overlay - Show real-time wind speed/direction on the hole map, with a "plays-like" adjusted distance factoring in wind + elevation.
- Smart club suggestion - Based on your personal club distances, wind, elevation, and lie, suggest which club to hit.

## Stats, Analytics, and Strokes Gained

- Per-round and historical stats dashboards with charts and trends over time.
- Tracking of key stats: FIR (fairways in regulation), GIR (greens in regulation), putts, sand saves, up-and-downs, penalties, chips, bunker shots, and more.
- Club distance tracking: record shot distances per club and build personal distance averages.
- Dispersion maps - Plot where shots land relative to the target. Shows patterns like "I always miss right with my 7-iron."
- Hole-by-hole insights and course-specific stats so you can analyze performance on particular courses or holes.
- Advanced analytics modules and strokes-gained style stats in higher tiers (compare against benchmarks, identify strengths/weaknesses).
- Strokes gained breakdown by category - Off the tee, approach, around the green, putting. Show which area costs the most strokes vs. a chosen benchmark (scratch, 10-handicap, 20-handicap).
- Practice-to-round correlation - Log practice sessions (range, putting green, short game) and correlate practice frequency with stat improvements over time.
- "What if" simulator - "If you eliminated all 3-putts, your handicap would drop by X." Helps prioritize what to work on.

## Handicap, Rounds History, and Benchmarking

- Official or unofficial handicap calculation; in some cases direct linking/posting to USGA or similar handicap systems.
- Round history with full scorecards, stats, and improvement charts (scores, FIR/GIR, putts, penalties, etc.).
- Benchmarks and community comparisons (how your stats compare to similar-level golfers).

## Social, Games, and Off-Course Extras

- Games and side-bets: Skins, Stableford, match play, Nassau, and other formats with automatic calculations.
- Live leaderboards for your group or events, plus multi-group or "tournament" style scoring.
- Social feed: share scorecards, photos, and videos, follow friends, comment, and get notifications about friends' rounds.
- Challenges and global leaderboards with badges or prizes from brands.
- Tee-time integrations (e.g., GolfNow offers) and some coaching/game-improvement content.

## AI Caddie and Coaching

- Post-round AI debrief - After each round, generate a natural-language summary of performance, highlighting where strokes were lost and what to focus on.
- Personalized drill recommendations - Based on weak stats, suggest specific practice drills with videos/instructions.
- Course strategy planner - Before playing a new course, generate a hole-by-hole game plan based on your distances and tendencies.

## Weather and Conditions

- Round conditions tagging - Log weather (temp, wind, rain), course conditions (wet/firm), and tee box used. Filter stats by conditions to see how you play in different scenarios.
- Lightning/weather alerts - Push notifications for incoming storms with time-to-shelter estimates.

## Equipment and Gear

- Bag setup tracking - Log which 14 clubs are in the bag per round. Track performance changes when you swap equipment.
- Club lifecycle - Track how many rounds/shots each club has seen. Notify when grips are due for replacement or when wedge grooves may be worn.

## Gamification and Engagement

- Achievement system - Unlock badges for milestones: first birdie, first eagle, break 100/90/80, 10 rounds tracked, play 5 different courses, etc.
- Streak tracking - Consecutive rounds under a score, consecutive FIR, consecutive pars, etc.
- Season goals - Set targets (e.g., "break 85 by September", "60% FIR average") with progress tracking and reminders.
- "Golden shot" highlights - Mark your best shot of the round with GPS pin + optional photo. Build a personal highlight reel over time.

## Data and Integration

- Scorecard OCR - Snap a photo of a paper scorecard and auto-digitize it for rounds played without the app.
- Export and API - Export data to CSV/PDF, or provide an API so users own their data and can build their own dashboards.
- Wearable heart rate integration - Correlate heart rate/stress data with performance.
